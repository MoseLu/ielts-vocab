## Style Prompt

雅思学习绘本风格的移动端贴纸资产库：奶油白纸感、深棕手绘线、柔和粉绿橙点缀、轻微纸张纹理。猫咪像一位温柔的 IELTS 学习小导师，带有完整头身、表情和学习动作；整体可爱但不幼稚，像贴在学习笔记、词卡、听力材料和错词本上的透明贴纸。

## Typography

- HyperFrames previews may use system rounded sans fonts.
- Final sticker PNGs must not include Chinese UI copy or baked instructional text.

## Asset Rules

- Export final assets as transparent PNGs with generous padding.
- Cat tutor v1 derives from the high-fidelity login cat style, but final exported tutor cats must remove flags, flagpoles, event-mascot gestures, and other non-learning props.
- Keep linework warm, imperfect, and hand-drawn; avoid geometric icon-style cats.
- Use full-body or clearly posed cat artwork for cat assets; no floating cat-head-only mascot for primary placements.
- Reusable learning objects should be recognizable at small mobile sizes.
- Decorative stickers are hidden from accessibility in React Native unless explicitly used as functional controls.

## Motion Rules

- HyperFrames only creates preview and animation master files.
- React Native consumes final PNGs or MP4s; do not embed HyperFrames runtime in the app.
- Motion should be subtle: breathing cat, dangling letter, soft leaf drift, and button-sticker bobbing.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not copy or trace 柠檬轻断食 assets, resource names, poses, or compositions.
- Do not use stock photo, glossy 3D, generic emoji, or clean vector icon aesthetics.
- Do not bake UI labels, Chinese text, watermarks, fake lorem text, or brand marks into stickers.
- Do not use purple-blue gradients or dark tech palettes.
- Do not reintroduce the old SVG-path cat as a product mascot.
- Do not use flag-holding cats or celebration mascots as default learning companions.
